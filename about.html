About Me

A brief Introduction...

BIO

I began my career in Aviation in 1991 and have worked primarily as an IFR controller in Oceanic, High and Low Enroute environemnts with NAV CANADA. I've been an Instructor at the national training facility, an OJI, and currently work as the Technical Services Manager for the Moncton FIR.

Music

I've always had a passion for music and taught myself to play numerous instruments over the past 40 years. I've been a member of several local bands, and have even been known to sing once or twice.

Art

I recently discovered the pre-retirement hobby of oil painting. I've taken a lot of instruction from various sources and truly enjoy the relaxation it brings. You can see some of my art here.

Programming

Computer sciences have been a hobby of mine since high school. I am not a developer by any means, but I do continuously try to learn new languages. In fact, this website was built by me. Some people find coding to be tedious, but the thrill of making it work is exciting to me!
